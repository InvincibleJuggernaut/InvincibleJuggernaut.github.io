Real Madrid is the stuff of legends. Considered to be a supreme force in club football, no other club can claim to have such an influence over both fans and players like Real Madrid can. Founded on 6 March 1902 as the Madrid Football Club, the club has traditionally worn a white home kit since inception. The word real is Spanish for "royal" and was bestowed to the club by King Alfonso XIII in 1920 together with the royal crown in the emblem. The team has played its home matches in the 81,044-capacity Santiago Bernabéu Stadium in downtown Madrid since 1947. Unlike most European sporting entities, Real Madrid's members (socios) have owned and operated the club throughout its history. The club is one of the most followed clubs all over the world. The club holds many long-standing rivalries, most notably El Clásico with Barcelona and El Derbi with Atlético Madrid.

This is what Franz Beckenbauer had to say about the club, even though he never played for them.

This is what Sergio Ramos, the current team captain, had to say about the club.
